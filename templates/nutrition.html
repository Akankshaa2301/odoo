Essential Nutrients for a Healthy Body

Nutrient Function Sources
Vitamins Essential for various metabolic processes and overall health. Fruits, vegetables, dairy products, meats.
Minerals Important for bone health, fluid balance, and muscle function. Nuts, seeds, dairy products, leafy greens.
Proteins Builds and repairs tissues, and is a crucial part of enzymes and hormones. Meat, fish, eggs, legumes, nuts.
Carbohydrates Main source of energy for the body. Bread, rice, pasta, fruits, vegetables.
Fibers Essential for digestive health. Whole grains, fruits, vegetables, legumes.
